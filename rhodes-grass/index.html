Rhodes Grass – Premium Quality for Maximum Animal Performance

At Gulf Harvest, we take pride in sourcing and supplying some of the finest Rhodes Grass seeds available globally. Known for its exceptional adaptability and nutritional value, Rhodes Grass has become one of our core strengths in the forage seed segment.

Why Rhodes Grass?

Rhodes Grass is a perennial, highly palatable, and extremely productive forage option that delivers consistent results across a wide range of climates and soil types. It is suitable for:

Dairy and beef cattle

Sheep and goats

Camels and equine species

Dairy and beef cattle

Whether grown in permanent pastures or short-to-medium-term ley pastures, Rhodes Grass provides reliable feed quality and quantity.

Discover More Discover More

Agronomic & Soil Benefits

Rhodes Grass is not just a feed solution—it is a soil builder. Its deep root structure helps in:

Improving organic matter

Restoring soil structure

Reducing harmful nematode populations

Enhancing long-term soil fertility

Diploid Rhodes Grass

Our diploid varieties are known for:

Rapid response to moisture and temperature
High yielding capacity with fast regrowth
Strong water-use efficiency
Ability to withstand repeated cutting or grazing

Well-managed diploid Rhodes Grass can produce over 20 tons/ha of dry matter annually, with no oxalate or prussic acid issues, ensuring safe and dependable feed.

Tetraploid Rhodes Grass

For regions with higher rainfall and longer growing seasons, tetraploid varieties perform exceptionally well. They offer:

Later flowering cycles

Increased palatability through higher natural sugar content

Strong performance in extended seasons

The Ideal Choice for Livestock Farmers

Nutrient-dense, palatable, and highly productive—Rhodes Grass is one of the best forages for all ruminant animals. Its versatility and proven performance make it the preferred selection for commercial farms, dairy units, and feed producers worldwide.
